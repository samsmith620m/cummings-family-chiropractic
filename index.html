This is the CFC story...

Chris P Cummings, DC, is a Chiropractic specialist in Cedar Rapids, Iowa. He graduated from Palmer College of Chiropractic — Davenport in 1998, has over 20 years of diverse experience in the field, and specializes in Chiropractic services. Dr. Cummings also coordinates with other doctors and physicians in medial groups including Cummings Family Chiropractic Pc.

Cummings Family Chiropractic, located in Cedar Rapids, Iowa is the area’s leading chiropractic clinic serving Cedar Rapids, Hiawatha, Marion, Palo, and the surroundings areas since 1998. We specialize in gentle, effective chiropractic adjustments, therapeutic exercises, computerized diagnostic testing, custom orthotics and much more. Our mission at Cummings Family Chiropractic is to partner with our patients to help you lead a happier, healthier life and to be a positive resource for our community’s health through personalized, family-based, extraordinary chiropractic care.
